How it works

Your rating,
earned on court.

01

Log your match

Record the result after every game — singles or doubles, padel or tennis. Takes under 30 seconds. The more you log, the more accurate your rating becomes.

02

Your Elo updates

Win against a stronger player and your rating climbs more. Beat someone weaker and it barely moves. Honest, dynamic, fair — the same system used in chess and football rankings.

03

Know your level

See exactly where you stand on the leaderboard. Find opponents at your level. Track your progress over time with a rating that means something.

Why Elo?

The same system used in chess, table tennis, and football rankings. It adjusts based on the strength of your opposition — so beating a top-rated player matters significantly more than beating a beginner. Your rating is a true reflection of your game, not just your win count.

About RALLY

Built by players,
for players.

Padel is the fastest growing sport in the world. In Spain alone, there are over 5 million players and thousands of clubs — yet there is still no universal way to know your level. No shared rating. No objective benchmark. Just a game that keeps growing with no infrastructure to match it.

RALLY was Sascha's idea. He brought Iñaki on to lead the commercial side — and together they're building the performance identity layer for padel, starting in Madrid in 2026.

We're launching with a small group of founding players who'll help shape what RALLY becomes. If you care about your game, we'd love you to be part of it.
